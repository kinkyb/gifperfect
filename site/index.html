How it works

A few controls. One click.

No configuration files. No command line. No video encoding knowledge required.

1

Drop your video

Drag and drop any MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV or WebM file onto the app.

2

Set target size

Pick a preset (15 / 25 / 99 MB) or type any custom value. GIF Perfect figures out the chunk length automatically.

3

Choose resolution & FPS

480p to original, 15–30 fps. Optionally extract JPG frames every N seconds as timestamped JPGs alongside your GIFs.

4

Get your GIFs

Output folder opens automatically. Each GIF is guaranteed under your target size.

FAQ

Common questions

What video formats does GIF Perfect support?
MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WebM, and M4V. If FFmpeg can open it, GIF Perfect can convert it.
Does it need an internet connection?
Only to validate your licence key on first launch (and occasionally after). All conversion processing is 100% local — your video never leaves your machine.
How does the target size chunking work?
GIF Perfect converts a short test segment of your video to estimate the output bitrate (MB/s). It then calculates the maximum chunk duration that stays under your target size and splits the full video into chunks of that length.
What is the free tier limit?
The free tier allows 3 conversions per day. Output GIFs include a full-image watermark tiled across the frame. Purchase a Creator or Studio licence to remove it and get unlimited conversions.
Is FFmpeg included or do I need to install it?
FFmpeg is bundled inside the GIF Perfect installer. You don't need to install anything else.
Can I use one licence on multiple machines?
Yes — your licence covers personal use on your Mac and Windows machines.
What platforms have GIF size limits?
Size limits and GIF acceptance vary widely by platform. See the full reference at gifperfect.com/platforms.
